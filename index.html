About Me

The little bits

  • December 1989

    A Simple Package

    I was born to a loving family weird enough to put me in a mail box and photograph it. Throughout my childhood, I sought after nothing but physical activity and social interactions. As I matured, I noticed a significant change in my interests, drawing me closer to the technology of today and ultimately, coding.

  • March 2012

    Progress and Graduation

    The day I graduated from Basic Training and planned to make a difference in the world. I began focusing on technology and this was reinforced by my choice in MOS(Military Occupational Specialty), 13T. I specialized in Meterological Survey and Field Artillery. This allowed me to better my understanding of computers and how they affect the world around us. I then furthered my knowledge by attending the 25U school, specializing in Networking and Radio Communications. This has all led me to the same end-point, computing and coding.

  • May 2014

    Career Change

    After years of Construction, Installation and Arboriculture I decided to follow my passion and aquired a job with Microsoft. I greatly increased my knowledge of .NET and began a heavy interest in C++. I changed directions a few years later and primarily focused on my son and an after school program called AYS(At Your School). This has done wonders for my communication skills and most importantly patience.

  • January 2017

    Dreaming In Code

    In January of 2017, I attended Eleven Fifty Academy and could not have been more excited! We have focused on HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery and dove deeper into coding and design with each passing day. Within the small 12 week course, I grew as an adult and a scholar. With the knowledge I gained along the way, I can now combine my past experiences with the new and bring a whole new perspective to the game.

  • August 2017

    Current Endeavors

    After receiving my Javascript Certification, I searched high and low for a job in which I could express and grow my skills. I landed a wonderful job at Geodis where I'm currently an IT Coordinator with the responsibility of reviewing employee hours and performance through a Labor Management System. I then transfer that data to a pivot table for review. The reports I create improve overall account performance for every account I manage. I diligently work to improve my knowledge and plan on furthering my education for future endeavors.

Workout Log

Blood, Sweat and Tears.

The Workout Log is a simple application designed to record workout sessions whether they be specific routines or a light jog/activity. The Workout Log was created using Angular as well as an API to store user data. Angular and HTML alone are a nice change of pace when compared to MEAN or MERN. I would like to go back and change the application to work towards my personal needs while at the same time increasing my knowledge of Angular.
